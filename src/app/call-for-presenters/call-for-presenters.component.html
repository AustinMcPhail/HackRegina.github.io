Call for Presenters

Speaking at HackRegina Tech Talks

Would you like to speak at a HackRegina event? We welcome you! Of course, we have guidelines that we ask all of our presenters to follow. These guidelines ensure that our members receive value from every presentation, that the speakers receive value through interaction with our members and that our sponsors are well represented.

Speaker Guidelines

  1. You must LOVE tech as much as we do. This is the perfect opportunity to preach to the choir. We are a general tech community and welcome talks on a broad range of topics from deep dives in to a specific tech, to experiences with a new language or framework, to startup life and culture.
  2. No sales pitches. HackRegina is an informal group of professionals who get together every month because we want to, not as part of our jobs or career development. The meeting is definitely not meant as a means to be sold new products. So leave the sell sheets and the brochures at the office. Don’t call us, we’ll call you. Focus on making a good impression on a human level, rather than on a product level. Chances are, if we like you as a person, we’ll be more inclined to check out your product.
  3. Attend some of our meetings prior to presenting. This is arguably the best way to get a sense for the community you’ll be addressing. If you can’t make it to a meeting, try joining our #coffeecrew channel on slack where you'll get matched with a new member to meet up with every couple of weeks.
  4. We record many of our presentations on video. Videos are shared on the web. You’re welcome to use the video on your own blog or web site.
  5. Presentations last for 45 minutes. You can do 30 minutes of speaking followed by 15 minutes of Q & A, or you can use the entire 45 minutes interactively. Your call!

Typical Tech Talk Agenda

Most HackRegina tech talks follow this agenda:

  • [30 minutes] Networking + eating refreshments (if provided).
  • [45 minutes] Speaker presentation + Q&A
  • [? minutes] Hangout or head to a nearby restaurant/pub for after presentation conversation.
